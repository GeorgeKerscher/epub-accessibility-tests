MathML No Namespace Test

math-160 MathML No Namespace

The test checks the reading experience of MathML with no namespace declared.

The test passes if the following steps succeed.

  • The assistive technology or other reading technologies report presence of the MathML expression and provide functionality to read it.
  • If you are a visual user, then please verify that you are able to see the portions of the equation highlighted as you navigate the various parts of the equation.

The test fails if you don't read/hear the equation containing an equals sign and the presence of a fraction contained within.

Test 160

Math Equation will appear between the following two lines.

y − y 1 = y 2 − y 1 x 2 − x 1 ( x − x 1 )

Additional Notes for Test 160
